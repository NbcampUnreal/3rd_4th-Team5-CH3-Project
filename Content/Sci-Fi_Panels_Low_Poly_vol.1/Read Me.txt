Hey guys! Eugene Apekin and me are glad to introduce our cooperative project. 

This  low poly sci_fi panels was made to let you spend less time for modeling and more for your ready ideas.


Features:

10 Panels - 2x2
10 Panels - 2x4
Bonus -  pipes and pipes connectors. 
PBR textures for Unreal, Unity, Vray. Substance painter maps to create personal variant of material.
Texture Size: 4096*4096
LODs: No
Can be used to construct levels.
Models are saved in OBJ and FBX files.
